# Age-Calculator
Age Calculator created with HTML CSS and JS
---
The Website is made to Calculate how old you are in years, months and days. Just put in your birthday in the "TT.mm.jjjj" placeholder and press the "Calculate" button. Your exact Age will be displayed below your Birthdate.
---
Your age is calculate via JavaScript placed in the "index.html" file. It subtracts your birthday with the current day via ".getdate; .getMonth; .getFullYear" and some "if; else" prompts.
---
As you use the calender icon to fill in your Birthdate everything works fine but as soon your put in your fake Birthday manualy with your Keyboard you also are able to set a Date in the future. In this case it will display you a minus year.
---
Have fun calculate your age ;)
